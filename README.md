# FUTURE_CS_02
## Phishing Detection & Awareness Report

## Tools Used
- **Email Header Analysis Tools** – to inspect the sender, routing, and legitimacy of emails  
- **Web Browser** – to verify sender domains and links  
- **Text Editor (Notepad / VS Code)** – to view and save email samples  
- **Microsoft Word / Google Docs** – to create the report document (PDF/Doc)  

## Analysis Approach
1. **Collected phishing email samples** – Gathered realistic phishing, suspicious, and safe emails.  
2. **Analyzed email headers** – Checked sender authenticity, IP addresses, and email routing information.  
3. **Inspected sender domains and links** – Verified whether URLs were legitimate or malicious.  
4. **Identified phishing indicators** – Noted fake domains, urgent language, requests for sensitive information, and suspicious attachments.  
5. **Classified email risks** – Labeled each email as Safe, Suspicious, or Phishing based on identified indicators.  
6. **Documented findings clearly** – Organized all examples, indicators, and risk classifications into the report.  
7. **Created prevention guidelines** – Provided actionable tips and Do’s/Don’ts for users and employees.
